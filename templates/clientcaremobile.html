What should I do prior to my appointment?
• Use oil-free cleanser to remove all makeup on eyes and lashes. Also remove eye mascara.
• If you wear contact lens, we recommend removing them
• Reframe from curling or perming your natural lashes prior to receiving your full set.

Does the progress hurt?
No, it does not lashes are not attached to the eyelid. Lashes are attached on top of the natural lash not eyelid. So it would not prevent the original lash from growing.

How often do I need a refill?:
On average, refills are recommended every 2-3 weeks to keep the lashes looking their best.


Lash Artist Etiquette
“Lash artist will never ignore the health of clients natural lash” - Good lash artist will always adjust or make recommendation for clients
After Care Instructions:
Do not get them wet after application (wait for 24 hours after application) For the lash extensions to last longer,  try to not sleep on lashes. Avoid touching with finger tips because finger tip has oil.
No Mascara
The less you touch, the longer it stays!

Do not use oil base Cleansers. Clean Regularly. Use Foam or Gel Cleansers(brush it with the spoolie, rinse
with slow running water)

If you need eyeliner – draw on the outer corner of your eyes

Dry it with hair dryer after shower (warm or cold setting, blowing upwards)

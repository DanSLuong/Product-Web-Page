Light Eyes USA processes orders Monday through Friday, excluding major US holidays.

Orders will be processed within 1-2 business days. Orders placed on Saturday and Sunday will begin processing the following Monday.


Domestic Shipping (within U.S.A)

Standard Shipping: $8 3-5 Business Days

2 Day Shipping: $15 2 Business Days (Expedited orders must be placed before 1:00 PM PST M-F in order to be shipped the same day)

Overnight Shipping: $45 (Must be placed before 1:00 PM PST M-F in order to be shipped the same day)


Track your order

Light Eyes USA is not responsible for lost or stolen packages, so please track your package and try to be as available as possible when your order is supposed to arrive. Once you have received your shipping confirmation, USPS will have the most current information regarding your package.

Return Policy:

Your satisfaction is our number one priority. If in the unlikely event there is a defective product or quality concerns beyond your control, please return within 30 days of delivery to receive a refund or exchange. A return label will be provided. After 30 days, the product is non-refundable.

If you wish to return or exchange any item, you can do so for up to 30 days from invoice date for a refund or exchange. All refunds will be credited back to the original payment method. For returned items, items must be unopened and in the original package with receipt or proof of purchase. Light Eyes USA is not responsible for covering these shipping costs.

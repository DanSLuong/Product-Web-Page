Volume Eyelash Extensions
Specialty Volume Lashes (only available at Light Eyes USA Location) also known as Russian lashes.
Volume lashes is the use of multiple ultra fine lash extensions that are hand crafted and fanned at the time of application by the lash artist using tweezers. Despite having multiple extensions, the fan is still placed onto one individually isolated natural lash and results in great retention without damaging the natural lash. Volume, or 3D lashes, involve attaching multiple lashes to a single natural eyelash.

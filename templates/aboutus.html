Light Eyes USA is the only exclusive distributer of the Light Eyes Brand in United States. Our products are a beauty combination of being fashionable and looking natural; we specialize in manufacturing eyelash extension products, and beauty related products.

Our milestones is to create a product that helps improve lash artists efficiency, while making sure products are compliant to the health and safety of all our consumers, and also making our products more affordable while upholding and maintaining product quality; are all equally important.

Our distributor head office in Taiwan, CEO studies chemistry and is extremely familiar and knowledgeable with different types of ingredients and reactants, therefore consumers can be ensured that the ingredients in our products are carefully selected.


Our Founder
History
Light Eyes USA helps expands the brand to the United States since 2016. During 2016 Light Eyes Brand collaborated with our founder, Sherin Lee and started Light Eyes USA in San Jose, with the assistance of their staffs, establishment, and their marketing enabled us to build relationships and rapport with the public and build on becoming a more reputable company.

Over years of selling quality eyelash extension products, Light Eyes Brand has proven our quality of our products and customer service was the two main attractions that kept our valuable clienteles to continue to shop with us. Light Eyes Brand products are recognized and earned KC certification and the SGS certification in Taiwan since 2013. Light Eyes Brand was already a well-known reputable company in Asia with the help of word of mouth through our clienteles, and hopes to continue to expand worldwide.

Light Eyes Brand is available in United States, China, Hong Kong, Macao, Japan, Singapore, and Malaysia.
Sherin Lee is the founder of Light Eyes USA established Face-N-body since 2012, which is consistently rated 5 stars on yelp. She started Light Eyes USA in 2016, she brought the Light Eyes Brand over to the United States, and making the impossible, possible building the brand in United States.

Light Eyes USA was featured in Miss Asians America 2017, and exhibit in both Face & Body Expo in San Jose, and IECSC Vegas Show. Sherin Lee is also very meticulous in selecting products and brands and this is known to all her clienteles, she is known to test the products before she purchase or invest – she constantly endeavors to succeed by trying new products, changing strategies, and consistently upgrades and educates herself.

Due to business success within the last two years, Light Eyes USA switched from being a start up company to becoming a more reputable company. Therefore, 2018 we were able to lower our price to thank all our supporters and customers. Our founders mission is to educate, guide, and bring the lash market up while making our products more affordable.
